Freddie Mercury

The Legend Of Rock

Freddie Mercury is one of the most iconic figures in human history, whose name is recognised by almost everybody.

Here is a brief summary of his life events and accomplishments:

  • 1946 - Born in Zanzibar.
  • 1964 - Moved with his family to Middlesex, England.
  • 1969 - Graduates with a diploma in art and design at Ealing Art College. Joined Liverpool-based band Ibex, later renamed Wreckage, which played "very Hendrix-style, heavy blues".
  • 1970 - Formed Queen with guitarist Brian May and drummer Roger Taylor.
  • 1981 - Performed in the Morumbi Stadium in SÃ£o Paulo. The band was the first ever to play in South American stadiums, breaking worldwide records for concert attendance.
  • 1985 - Performed at Live Aid. It was one of his most legendary performances.
  • 1986 - Queen played behind the Iron Curtain when they performed to a crowd of 80,000 in Budapest, in what was one of the biggest rock concerts ever held in Eastern Europe.
  • 1986 - Mercury's final live performance with Queen at Knebworth Park in England, which drew an attendance estimated as high as 200,000.
  • 1990 - Queen was awarded the Brit Award for Outstanding Contribution to British Music.
  • 1991 - Died at age of 45 due to complications from AIDS.
